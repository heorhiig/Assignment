Introduction to Spiders

Spiders are arachnids found all over the world, known for their eight legs and ability to spin webs. They play a crucial role in ecosystems, controlling insect populations and serving as food for other animals.

“Despite their sometimes fearsome appearance, most spiders are harmless to humans. In fact, many are beneficial, reducing insect pests in natural and human habitats.”

For more fascinating information about spiders, you can check out this article from National Geographic.
